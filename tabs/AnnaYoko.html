DESIGNERS

Anna Myntekær & Yoko Maja

Anna Myntekær and Yoko Maja co-founded Bonnetje, a Copenhagen-based brand dedicated to upcycling old suits into new designs. With backgrounds in the fashion industry, they started the brand to blend creativity with sustainability, especially after becoming mothers. Their innovative approach has attracted attention at events like Copenhagen Fashion Week
