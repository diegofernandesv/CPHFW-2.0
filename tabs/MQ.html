Martin Quad is a Danish fashion brand debuting at Copenhagen Fashion Week SS24 with a unique opera-style presentation. Supported by the Danish Art Foundation and praised in Vogue US, the brand blends traditional craftsmanship with modern, unconventional designs. Martin Quad redefines fashion presentations with conceptual storytelling and innovative use of fabrics and techniques.
