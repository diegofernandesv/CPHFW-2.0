Rick Owens

Rick Owens is an influential American designer known for his eponymous brand, Rick Owens, founded in 1994. Celebrated for its dark, avant-garde style, the brand blends grunge, gothic, and high fashion elements, with unique silhouettes and luxury materials, making Owens a major figure in contemporary fashion.
