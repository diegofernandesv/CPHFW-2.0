Virgil Abloh

Virgil Abloh was an influential American designer known for his brand, Off-White, founded in 2012. Celebrated for blending streetwear with luxury, Off-White brought a fresh, urban aesthetic to high fashion. Abloh’s innovative approach and cultural impact, including his role as Louis Vuitton’s Artistic Director for menswear, established him as a key figure in contemporary fashion.
