Berner Kühl

Frederik Berner Kühl is the designer and founder of Berner Kühl, a sustainable menswear brand established in Copenhagen in 2020. He has a background in production and holds a Master's degree from Polimoda in Florence. Kühl's brand focuses on a modular wardrobe concept that encourages durability and mindful consumption, emphasizing high-quality materials in each garment​
